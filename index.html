About The Committee

DJS COMPUTE is a student-run committee under the Department of Computer Engineering of D.J. Sanghvi College of Engineering. Our primary goal is to foster a culture of technical innovation and collaboration among students. We aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry demands by organizing a variety of events. These include insightful seminars, hands-on workshops on cutting-edge technologies, competitive hackathons, and coding competitions that challenge and inspire our members. We strive to create a vibrant community where students can learn, grow, and build amazing things together.
